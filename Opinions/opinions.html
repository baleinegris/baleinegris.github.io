My top 10 Games:

#1 Hollow Knight The absolute GOAT. I love this game so much. #6 Factorio Optimizing is fun :>
#2 Divinity Original Sin 2 The best turn-based RPG there is. Close with BG3 but combat is better in DoS2. #7 Total War Warhammer 2 Epic over the top fantasy battles and endless content.
#3 Gwent: The Witcher Card Game Highest skill online card game, beautiful art. RIP Gwent support :( #8 Just Cause 3 Best flow and super fun casual game.
#4 Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice I only wanted to put one FromSoftware game on this list and Sekiro has the best combat system of any game. #9 Tetris This one is sorta weird but if you know me you know I'm kinda obsessed with Tetris.
#5 Borderlands 2 The OG looter shooter and my favourite game game as a kid. #10 Dead Cells I had to put a Roguelike somewhere on this list and Dead Cells is the best of them.
